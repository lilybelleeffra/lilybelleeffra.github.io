Valley Fever

Valley-Bottom Fever: The sudden, urgent need to get out of the valley
Using inspiration from the landscape I grew up in to create a new world that explores feelings of change, growing up and how moving away from your hometown gives you the chance to look back
Environment + film created in year 2 of my BA in Experimental Animation at London College of Communication
Created using Maya, Adobe Photoshop + Premiere Pro. Photography from me.
